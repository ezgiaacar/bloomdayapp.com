Privacy Policy

Your data belongs to you. Here is exactly what we collect, why, and how it is protected.

Last updated: March 2026
OverviewData We CollectHow We Use It AdvertisingThird PartiesStorage Your RightsChildrenContact
01 — Overview

Simple by design

BloomDay is a task planner and virtual garden app. We collect only what is necessary to run the app. We do not sell data, build profiles, or share your information with advertisers.

We believe privacy should be the default, not a setting you have to find. This policy is written to be read, not to obscure. If anything is unclear, write to us.

02 — Data We Collect

What we know about you

@

Account information

When you create an account, we collect your email address and a securely hashed password. This is used solely to authenticate your identity. We do not collect your name, phone number, or any other personal identifier.

☘

App data (local only)

Your tasks, garden progress, plant inventory, streak history, achievements, journal entries, time capsules, and preferences are stored locally on your device using on-device storage. None of this data is sent to or stored on our servers.

☁

Weather data

The app fetches your local weather once per day to apply real-world conditions to your garden. No location data is stored on our servers. It is used in the moment and discarded.

We do not collect device identifiers, IP addresses, usage statistics, crash telemetry, or any other form of analytics. Zero tracking. Zero fingerprinting.
03 — How We Use It

Only what runs the app

⚿

Authentication

Your email and credentials are used to create and protect your account via Supabase. We support email/password and Apple Sign In.

↻

Local persistence

Your tasks, plants, achievements, and progress are saved to your device's local storage so your garden persists between sessions. Data does not sync across devices or to the cloud.

⚙

Notifications

If you enable task reminders, the app schedules local notifications on your device. Notifications are never routed through a server. Daily and weekly recurring reminders fire locally.

✉

Account communications

We may use your email for critical account events only: password reset and account deletion confirmation. We do not send marketing emails, newsletters, or promotional content.

04 — Advertising

Ads on the free plan

The free plan includes occasional ads served by Google AdMob. Ads appear at most once per day on launch and are never shown during Focus Mode or while you are actively using the garden.

Non-personalised ads only

BloomDay requests non-personalised ads from Google AdMob. No behavioural profile is built on you through this app. Google receives no personal data from BloomDay.

Upgrading to any paid plan (Bloom, Forest, or Eden) removes all ads permanently.

05 — Third Parties

Who else touches your data

Supabase

Account authentication only. Stores your email and hashed password. No garden or task data is sent to Supabase.

GDPR compliant · SOC 2

RevenueCat

Subscription management via Apple App Store and Google Play. Receives a transaction token only.

No payment data shared

Google AdMob

Non-personalised ads on the free plan only. No tracking data shared.

Free plan only
No other third parties have access to your data. We do not use Facebook SDK, Amplitude, Mixpanel, Firebase Analytics, Sentry, or any analytics or tracking service.
06 — Storage & Retention

Where your data lives

●

Local storage

Your tasks, plants, streaks, achievements, journal entries, and all garden data are stored locally on your device. We do not store this data on our servers. Your email address is the only personal data we hold.

●

Deleting the app

Removing the app from your device deletes all local data permanently, including your garden, tasks, and progress. This cannot be recovered.

●

Account deletion

Settings → Delete Account removes your email from our authentication system and erases all local data. After deletion, we retain nothing.

07 — Your Rights

What you can ask of us

1

Access

Request confirmation of what personal data we hold. The only personal data on our servers is your email address.

2

Deletion

Delete your account in-app (Settings → Delete Account) or by emailing us. This removes your email from our authentication system. All garden and task data is stored locally on your device and is deleted when you remove the app.

3

Export

Export your task data anytime from Settings → Export Data. Downloads as a CSV file directly to your device. Since all data is local, you always have full control.

08 — Children

Not for children under 13

BloomDay is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

09 — Contact

Questions or requests

We respond to all privacy enquiries within 5 business days.
