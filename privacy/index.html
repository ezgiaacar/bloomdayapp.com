Privacy Policy

Your data belongs to you. Here is exactly what we collect, why, and how it is protected.

01 — Overview

Simple by design

BloomDay is a task tracker and virtual garden app. We collect only what is necessary to run the app. We do not sell data, build profiles, or share your information with advertisers.

We believe privacy should be the default, not a setting you have to find. This policy is written to be read, not to obscure. If anything is unclear, write to us.

02 — Data We Collect

What we know about you

Account information

When you create an account, we collect your email address and a securely hashed password. This is used solely to authenticate your identity. We do not collect your name, phone number, or any other personal identifier.

App data

Your tasks, garden progress, plant inventory, streak history, and preferences are stored to provide a seamless experience across sessions and devices.

Weather data

The app fetches your local weather once per day to apply real-world conditions to your garden. No location data is stored on our servers — it is used in the moment and discarded.

· · ·

We do not collect device identifiers, IP addresses, usage statistics, crash telemetry, or any other form of analytics.

03 — How We Use It

Only what runs the app

  • Authentication. Your email and credentials are used to create and protect your account via Supabase.
  • Sync. Your tasks, plants, and progress are saved so your garden persists across sessions and devices.
  • Notifications. If you enable task reminders, the app schedules local notifications on your device — not through a server.
  • Account communications. We may use your email for critical account events (password reset, deletion). We do not send marketing emails.
04 — Advertising

Ads on the free plan

The free plan includes occasional ads served by Google AdMob. Ads appear at most once per day on launch and are never shown during Focus Mode.

BloomDay requests non-personalised ads only. Google AdMob does not build a behavioural profile on you through this app. Upgrading to any paid plan removes all ads permanently.

05 — Third Parties

Who else touches your data

  • Supabase. Account authentication and database storage. GDPR compliant, SOC 2 Type II certified. Data stored in EU (Frankfurt).
  • RevenueCat. Subscription management via Apple App Store. Receives a transaction token only — no payment data passes our servers.
  • Google AdMob. Non-personalised ads on the free plan only.

No other third parties have access to your data. We do not use Facebook, Amplitude, Mixpanel, Firebase Analytics, Sentry, or any analytics service.

06 — Storage & Retention

Where your data lives

  • Active accounts. All data retained while your account exists. Nothing auto-deleted.
  • Deleting the app. Removing the app does not delete your account. But your saved data will be lost.
  • Account deletion. Settings → Delete Account permanently removes all data. Deletion is irreversible. Data purged from backups within 30 days.
07 — Your Rights

What you can ask of us

  • Correction. Request correction of any inaccurate information.
  • Deletion. Delete your account in-app or by contacting us.
  • Portability. Export your task data anytime from Settings → Export My Data.
08 — Children

Not for children under 13

BloomDay is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. Contact us to report any concerns.

09 — Contact
