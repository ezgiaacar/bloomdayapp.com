How BloomDay works

01 — Tasks

The root of everything

Every seed you earn, every plant that grows, every streak you build begins with completing a task. Tasks are the heartbeat of BloomDay.

1

Add a task

Tap the input bar, type your task, and press ADD. Assign a category: Work, Health, Home, Learn, Social, or Self.

2

Set a time window

Tap Time to assign a start time and duration. Tasks completed during their window earn +1 bonus seed.

3

Add a reminder

Set a notification to fire before your task begins. You can choose how many minutes ahead.

4

Add a note

Attach a short note to any task. Notes appear in italic beneath the task title so you can add context or details.

5

Complete it

Tap the circle to complete. You earn 2 seeds per task. Swipe left to postpone or delete.

The calendar scrolls 180 days into the past and 365 days into the future. Tap any day to see its tasks, or schedule tasks for future dates.

03 — Plants

Species, growth & full bloom

BloomDay features over 100 hand-illustrated botanical species across three environments. Each plant grows through watering, not task completion.

How plants grow

Plants grow exclusively through watering. Tasks earn the seeds to buy plants, but only water makes them grow. Each species has its own growth rate and watering interval.

Watering moods

Your plant's mood depends on how recently it was watered. Mood affects nothing mechanically but gives you a gentle visual cue.

Full bloom

When a plant reaches 100% growth, a bloom celebration appears with a reward: +20 seeds and +1 Resilience Token. This happens only once per species.

04 — Garden

Three worlds to design

Your garden is a living reflection of your productivity. Arrange plants, add decor, and switch between themes.

1

Switch environments

Interior (houseplants), Garden (outdoor flowers and trees), Allotment (vegetables and herbs). Each has its own species catalog and visual scene.

2

Choose a theme

Scroll the theme bar to pick Cosy Cottage, Zen Studio, Nordic Loft, and more. Some themes require a paid plan.

3

Buy from the nursery

Browse the full species catalog. Tap a plant to see its cost, tier, latin name, and trivia. Spend seeds to add it to your collection.

4

Arrange your space

Tap ARRANGE to drag plants and decor items freely. Long-press to remove an item from the scene.

5

Share your garden

Tap SHARE GARDEN to capture a screenshot with your stats and the BloomDay watermark. Share it anywhere.

05 — Focus Mode

A commitment to deep work

Focus Mode is a full-screen timer with ambient sound, breathing animation, and bonus seed rewards.

⏱

Choose your duration

Select 25, 45, 60, or 90 minutes. A breathing plant animation keeps you grounded.

♪

Ambient sound

Toggle ambient garden sounds on or off. The sound loops gently throughout your session.

✓

Earn +3 seeds on completion

When the timer finishes, you receive 3 seeds automatically. Ending early forfeits the reward.

If you try to navigate away or end a session early, BloomDay asks for confirmation. Cancel returns you to the timer; your session continues uninterrupted.

07 — Achievements

Milestones that celebrate you

BloomDay tracks over 35 achievements across six categories. When you unlock a new achievement, a special celebration alert appears with a +5 seed reward.

TasksStreaksGarden WateringEconomySpecial

Achievements include milestones like completing your first task, reaching a 30-day streak, collecting 10 plants, watering for 7 consecutive days, reaching 200 seeds, completing your first focus session, and many more. View your full progress in Settings.

Achievements are detected automatically in real-time. The moment you hit a milestone, the alert appears. No need to check manually.

09 — Weather

Real conditions, real effects

BloomDay reads your local weather once per day. Weather conditions have mechanical effects on your garden, not just decoration.

☔

Rain or storm

Garden and allotment plants are automatically watered for free. Your watering drops are preserved.

☀

Sunny days

Clear skies give a gentle growth boost. Outdoor plants respond well to sunlight.

☁

Seasons

Spring (March–May) doubles all seed earnings from tasks. The season indicator appears in your garden scene.

10 — Recurring Tasks

Habits that repeat

Set any task to repeat daily or weekly. Recurring tasks reappear automatically on the correct days without you needing to recreate them.

1

Set frequency

When adding a task, choose Once, Daily, or Weekly. Daily tasks appear every day. Weekly tasks appear on the same weekday you created them.

2

Per-day tracking

Each recurring instance tracks completion independently. Completing Monday's instance doesn't affect Tuesday's.

3

Stop repeating

Swipe left on any recurring task to reveal the Stop button. This converts it to a one-time task and cancels its notification.

Recurring tasks get their own notification schedules. A daily task fires its reminder every day at the same time. A weekly task fires on the same weekday.
11 — Journal & Time Capsules

Letters to your future self

BloomDay includes a journal and a time capsule system, both connected to your plants.

Journal

Write daily reflections in the journal section under Settings. Each entry is timestamped and stored locally. Free users can save up to 3 entries; premium users get unlimited.

Time Capsules

1

Write a letter

Choose a plant and write a letter to your future self. The letter is sealed to that plant.

2

Grow your plant

The capsule stays sealed while the plant grows. You cannot read it until the plant reaches 100% growth.

3

Read your letter

When the plant fully blooms, the capsule opens automatically. A special reveal animation shows your letter alongside the bloomed plant.

Why this matters

The capsule creates a tangible link between your present effort and a future reward that only patience can unlock. It cannot be bought, skipped, or rushed.

14 — Tips

Getting the most from your garden

✿

Water every day

Growth comes only from watering. Open the app, water your plant, and let the streak build naturally.

⏱

Use Focus Mode for deep work

Every completed focus session earns +3 seeds. Use it for your hardest tasks of the day.

★

Save for rare plants

Forest and Eden tier plants cost 200–400 seeds. Saving for them should feel earned, not effortless.

☘

Guard your streak

A streak only requires one task per day. On slow days, complete your smallest task. That still counts.

✎

Write a time capsule early

Seal a letter to a slow-growing plant. By the time it blooms, you will have forgotten what you wrote. That surprise is the reward.

↻

Set up recurring tasks

Your daily habits should be recurring tasks. Set them once, and they reappear automatically every day or week.

The BloomDay philosophy

Every reward should be a consequence of real work, not a shortcut around it. Growth is quiet. It happens one task, one day, one leaf at a time.
