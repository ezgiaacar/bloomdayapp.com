01 — Tasks

The root of everything

Every seed you earn, every plant that grows, every streak you build — it all starts with completing a task.

1

Add a task

Tap the input bar, type your task, and press ADD. Assign a category: Work, Health, Home, Learn, Social, or Self.

2

Set a time window (optional)

Tap Time to assign a start time and duration. Tasks completed during their window earn +1 bonus seed.

3

Set a reminder

Tap the 🔔 icon on any task to get a notification 5 minutes before it begins.

4

Complete it — earn seeds

Tap the circle to complete. You earn 2 seeds per task. One reward per task per day.

02 — Seeds

The currency of your garden

Seeds are earned through consistent work and spent on plants. They are designed to feel valuable — not abundant.

03 — Plants

Species, growth & full bloom

How plants grow

Plants grow through watering alone. Tasks earn seeds to buy plants — not to grow them. Water your plant once every 20 hours. Sunny weather adds +1 bonus growth on your next water.

Watering moods

Watered today → Thriving · 1 day missed → Happy · 2 days → Thirsty · 3 days → Wilting · 4+ days → Critical

Full bloom

When a plant reaches 100% growth, a bloom moment appears with a reward: +20 seeds and +1 Resilience Token. This happens only once per species.

04 — Focus Mode

A commitment contract

Select a duration (25, 45, 60, or 90 min) and tap FOCUS. During the session:

✓

+1 bonus seed per task

Every task earns 3 seeds instead of 2 during Focus.

✓

One task visible at a time

Removes visual noise and forces sequential attention.

✓

Task input locked

You cannot add new tasks during a Focus session.

05 — Resilience Tokens

Earned, never bought

Tokens cannot be purchased. They are earned only through genuine milestones.

Use tokens to restore a broken streak or revive a critical plant (4+ days unwatered).

06 — Weather

Real conditions, real effects

BloomDay reads your local weather once per day. Weather has mechanical effects — not just decoration.

🌧

Rain or Storm

Garden and allotment plants are automatically watered for free.

☀️

Hot days (>28°C)

Outdoor plants wilt faster if not watered.

07 — Garden

Arrange, tend, share

1

Switch environments

Interior (houseplants), Garden (outdoor), Allotment (vegetables). Each has its own species.

2

Choose a theme

Scroll the theme bar — Cosy Cottage, Zen Studio, Nordic Loft and more. Some require a paid plan.

3

Arrange your space

Tap ARRANGE to drag plants and décor. Long-press to remove an item.

4

Share your garden

Tap SHARE GARDEN to capture a screenshot with your stats and BloomDay watermark.

08 — Weekly Report

Seven days, clearly seen

Every week BloomDay shows: tasks done, streak, days watered, challenge wins, completion rate, daily bar chart, and category breakdown. The mood headline changes based on your actual completion rate.

09 — Tips

Getting the most from your garden

🌿

Water every day

Growth comes only from watering. Open the app, water your plant, and let the streak build.

⏱

Use Focus Mode for deep work

Every task in Focus earns +1 bonus seed. Use it for your hardest tasks.

💰

Save for rare plants

Forest-tier plants cost 200–280 seeds. Saving for them should feel earned.

🔥

Guard your streak

A streak only requires one task per day. On slow days, complete your smallest task.

The app is designed around one principle: every reward should be a consequence of real work, not a shortcut around it.
