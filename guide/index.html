01 — Tasks

The root of everything

Every seed you earn, every plant that grows, every streak you build — it all starts with completing a task.

1

Add a task

Tap the + button, type your task, and press ADD. Assign a category: Work, Health, Home, Learn, Social, or Self.

2

Set a time and reminder

Assign a start time and duration. Toggle the reminder to get a notification 5 minutes before it begins.

3

Use templates

Tap Quick Templates to add a full routine at once — Morning Ritual, Study Block, Cat Care, Dog Care, and more.

4

Complete it — earn seeds

Tap the circle to complete. You earn 2 seeds per task, once per task per day. Toggling done/undone won't give extra seeds.

02 — Seeds

The currency of your garden

Seeds are earned through consistent work and spent on plants and decor. They are designed to feel valuable — not abundant.

ActionSeedsNotes
Complete a task+2Base rate. Once per task per day.
Daily Challenge+3–10One per day. Varies by difficulty.
Plant reaches full bloom+20One-time reward per plant.
Seeds do not expire. They accumulate across days. Some rare plants take several days of consistent work to afford — that tension is intentional.
03 — Plants

Species, growth & full bloom

How plants grow

Plants grow only through watering — not by completing tasks. Tasks earn seeds to buy plants; watering is what makes them grow. Each watering adds 5% growth toward full bloom.

Watering schedule
High-need plants (ferns, lettuce): every day. Standard (monstera, pothos): every 3 days. Low-need (cactus, snake plant): every 5 days. A water droplet label appears above any plant that needs watering.

Watering moods

Each plant's mood reflects how recently it was watered. Neglected plants visually wilt in the garden — they become transparent and tilt.

Thriving Happy Thirsty Wilting Needs Rescue

Full bloom

When a plant reaches 100% growth, a bloom moment appears with a reward: +20 seeds and +1 Resilience Token. This happens only once per plant. The "Growing Now" display on your Today screen automatically switches to the next unfinished plant.

04 — Focus Mode

A commitment to deep work

Tap the Focus card on the Today screen to enter a full-screen immersive session. Select a duration (25, 45, 60, or 90 min) and tap BEGIN.

Full-screen dark interface

Calming, distraction-free. Your active plant is displayed with a breathing animation alongside a large countdown timer.

Ambient sound

Toggle optional background audio for extra immersion during your session.

Stay focused

Leaving the app sends a notification to return. Tapping the close button or switching tabs asks you to confirm ending the session.

05 — Resilience Tokens

Earned, never bought

Tokens cannot be purchased. They are earned only through genuine milestones.

MilestoneTokens
7-day streak+1
14-day streak+1
30-day streak+1
100-day streak+1
Plant reaches full bloom+1

Tokens are capped at 10. Use them to restore a broken streak — a prompt appears automatically when you return after a missed day.

06 — Weather

Real conditions, real effects

BloomDay reads your local weather once per day. Weather has mechanical effects — not just decoration.

Rain or Storm

Garden and allotment plants are automatically watered for free.

Sunny day

+1 growth bonus on your next manual watering.

Fog & Snow

Visual overlays change the garden atmosphere — misty mornings and snow-dusted shelves.

07 — Garden

Arrange, tend, share

1

Switch environments

Interior (houseplants), Garden (outdoor), Allotment (vegetables). Each has its own species, themes, and decor.

2

Choose a theme

Scroll the theme bar — Cosy Cottage, Zen Studio, Nordic Loft, Grand Manor, and more. Some require a paid plan.

3

Buy plants and decor

Browse the Nursery tab for plants and the Decor tab for decorative items. Each has a seed cost.

4

Arrange your space

Tap ARRANGE to drag plants and decor to reposition them. Tap the X badge to remove an item.

5

Share your garden

Tap SHARE GARDEN to capture a screenshot with your stats, streak, and weather.

08 — Weekly Report

Seven days, clearly seen

The Progress tab shows your weekly performance at a glance.

Daily breakdown chart
Bar chart with done/total counts per day. Today highlighted in brass, perfect days in green. Weekly completion percentage shown alongside.
Highlights & categories
Perfect days count, best day of the week, top categories breakdown, and a mood headline that adapts to your actual completion rate.
09 — Tips

Getting the most from your garden

Water every day

Growth comes only from watering. Look for the water labels on your plants in the garden.

Use Focus Mode

The full-screen experience helps you stay present. Enable ambient sound for extra immersion.

Save for rare plants

Forest-tier plants cost 200–280 seeds. Eden-tier can reach 400–500. Let it feel earned.

Guard your streak

A streak only needs one task per day. On slow days, do your smallest task. Miss a day? Use a token.

Growing Now

The plant on your Today screen automatically switches to your next unfinished plant once the current one reaches full bloom. Keep growing.

The app is designed around one principle: every reward should be a consequence of real work, not a shortcut around it.
