The Experience

Not a book. Not a game. Something in between.

i.

A scene arrives

Each day, a new moment from the story lands in your hands. Brief. Intimate. Meant to be savored.

ii.

You make a choice

Forward or inward. To speak or stay silent. Small decisions that feel weightless — until they don't.

iii.

The story remembers

Over 7, 14, or 28 days, your choices accumulate. The narrative bends. The ending becomes yours.
